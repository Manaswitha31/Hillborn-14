One-Day Introduction to Design Thinking Workshop

Design thinking is a proven and repeatable approach to problem solving that can be applied to any challenge, regardless of size or industry. This approach differs from traditional problem solving in that it is grounded in understanding the human frictions and frustrations underlying any problem. By understanding the real, human issues provoking a problem, participants are able to quickly and objectively measure the effectiveness of different solutions.

In this one day course, participants will have the opportunity to:

  • Complete a full design cycle that results in a real, viable solution
  • Learn to quickly understand a problem and its human causes
  • Unlock dormant creativity through rapid ideation
  • Measure the effectiveness of solutions through testing

Employing this process in solving problems large and small results in more accurate, efficient, and viable solutions - saving organizations time, money, and manpower.
