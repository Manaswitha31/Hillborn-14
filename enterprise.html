Enterprise UX

Rocket designs sophisticated user experiences for essential and complex products used by professionals. For 25-years Rocket has been creating leading edge enterprise user experience designs, proudly contributing to our customers competitive advantage.

Nearly all commercial companies with software development teams have adopted DevOps, and the more advanced companies are deploying Design Operations (DesignOps) as a key component of their DevOps strategies. DesignOps is everything needed to support the act of designing. Professional DesignOps typically includes the recruiting and management of a design team, professional development and training, pre-approved sets of design tools, a User Experience Design System (UXDS), application design, and code templates. Effective DesignOps provides the foundation for designers and developers to move faster and produce consistent UX in their applications – saving time on both the development and on end-user training.

Rocket has applied this understanding to create a set of tools, processes, and frameworks that together form a technology-based system that allows DesignOps to be applied at scale in organizations wishing to quickly employ and benefit from improved software design. We call this commercial solution “Athena.”
